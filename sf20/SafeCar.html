Car Safety

What makes a car safe?

There are many things that can make a car safe or unsafe, here are some things to look out for when getting a car (This applies more if the car is second hand):

    Things to look out for when buying a car:

  • Make of Car
  • Age of Car
  • Is the car second hand?

How can i make sure my car is safe?

Before every drive it is best to check your mirrors and lights and a car shoud be serviced regularly.

    Common vehicle factors for crashes
