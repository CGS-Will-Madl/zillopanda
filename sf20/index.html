Brief Overview

In order to remain as safe as possible while driving one should try and avoid as many distractions as possible, passengers should respect the driver and the driver should have mobile devices turned off.

The driver should also respect the environment they find themselves in, if the roads are wet or icy they should slow down to increase their time to stop.
